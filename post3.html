Assignment 2: Empathy Machine - Houndview

12 February 2019

Motivation

The domesticated dog is often referred to as the human's best friend and plays an important role in the daily lives of many people. Dogs are often considered and treated by their guardians as quasi-humans and members of the family, thus being attributed human-like characteristics. This anthropomorphic view is extremely problematic, as it leads to misinterpretation of the pet‚Äôs behaviour, which may lead to incorrect or inappropriate treatment.


Concept

By using different kind of media, Houndview tries to apply the concept of "animal-centric-anthropomorphism", let guardians take the perspective of their animal and, in particular, bring guaridans closer to the reality of their pets' lives - with the aim of improving the specific understanding of the species on the part of guardians, increasing empathy towards their pets and, ultimately, increasing their quality of life. In order to achieve this, the dog's sense of sight is translated into a format that can be experienced by humans. In this way, the guardian can take on the perspective of his dog, re-experience the environment already familiar to him, and draw conclusions about his behaviour and his role in the relationship to his pet.
